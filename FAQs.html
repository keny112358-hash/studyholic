MCAT Frequently Asked Questions

What identification is required on test day?

Bring a valid government-issued photo ID. No phones or electronics. Follow official AAMC rules.

Are there limits on retaking the MCAT?

Max 3 times per year, 2 times in 4 years, 7 times lifetime. Plan attempts strategically.

How should I use practice tests?

Use official AAMC tests timed. Analyze errors. Simulate test day. Focus on understanding mistakes.

Which topics should I focus on?

Prioritize high-yield content. Strengthen scientific reasoning. Review weak areas identified in practice tests.

What should I bring on test day?

ID, registration confirmation, pencils, erasers, approved snacks. No electronics in testing area.
