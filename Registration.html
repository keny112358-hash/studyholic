How to Register

Visit the AAMC MCAT registration system, choose your test date and location, fill in your details, and submit payment.

Tip: Seats fill up quickly, especially for early dates. Log in early on registration day.

Understanding the Deadlines

60 Day Deadline: The earliest registration deadline. Canceling or rescheduling by this date provides the highest refund and flexibility.

30 Day Deadline: The middle registration deadline. You can still make changes, but the refund is smaller and test center choices may be limited.

10 Day Deadline: The final cutoff to make any registration changes or cancellations. After this date, no adjustments are allowed.

There are multiple deadlines because the AAMC provides increasing fees and fewer refund options as you get closer to the test date. Registering early gives you more control over where and when you take your MCAT in 2026.
